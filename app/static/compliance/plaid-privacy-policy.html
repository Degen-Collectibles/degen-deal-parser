Degen Collectibles Plaid Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

This policy explains how Degen Collectibles uses financial account data obtained through Plaid for the Degen Collectibles operations portal.

Purpose of the Integration

The Plaid integration is used for internal bank-feed import, bookkeeping reconciliation, cash-flow review, and operational financial reporting. Authorized staff use imported account and transaction data to reconcile bank activity against internal sales, inventory, expenses, payouts, and bookkeeping records.

Plaid Data We May Access

Depending on the financial institution connection and the consent granted, the application may access account metadata, balances, transaction history, transaction descriptions, transaction dates, merchant information, and related account identifiers needed for reconciliation. The application does not use Plaid data to initiate payments, make lending decisions, provide investment advice, or sell consumer financial information.

How Data Is Used

  • Import bank transactions into the bookkeeping reconciliation workflow.
  • Match bank activity against internal sales, payouts, fees, expenses, and inventory records.
  • Generate internal cash-flow, reconciliation, and exception reports.
  • Investigate sync errors, duplicate rows, and reconciliation mismatches.

Consent and Control

A bank connection must be authorized through Plaid Link. Authorized Degen Collectibles administrators should only connect accounts they are permitted to administer for company bookkeeping. Connections can be revoked through Plaid, the connected financial institution, or Degen Collectibles support.

Data Sharing

Degen Collectibles does not sell Plaid data. Plaid data is used only for the benefit of Degen Collectibles internal bookkeeping and operational workflows. Access is limited to authorized personnel and service providers that operate the application infrastructure under Degen Collectibles control, or when disclosure is required by law.

Data Storage and Security

Production traffic is served over HTTPS. Access to bookkeeping and finance features is role-based and limited to authorized staff. Runtime credentials and API secrets are stored outside source code. Plaid access tokens are stored in encrypted form and are revoked or rotated if compromise is suspected.

Retention and Deletion

Imported bank-feed records are retained only as needed for bookkeeping, tax, audit, legal, and operational purposes. See the Plaid data deletion and disconnect instructions.

Contact

Privacy and data requests can be sent to info@degencollectibles.com.
