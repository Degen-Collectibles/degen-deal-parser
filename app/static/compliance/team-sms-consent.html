Degen Team SMS Consent and Terms

Effective date: May 21, 2026

Degen Collectibles uses SMS for the Degen Team employee portal. Messages are sent only to employees, staff, and new hires for team portal access, account security, and internal operational notifications.

No marketing texts: This SMS program is not used for promotional campaigns, customer outreach, or public marketing.

How Employees Opt In

Managers or administrators collect employee mobile phone numbers during onboarding, employee profile setup, or team portal account setup. Employees may also enter or confirm their mobile number in the Degen Team portal. By providing a mobile number for team portal access, employees consent to receive operational SMS from Degen Collectibles at that number.

Employees may also request password reset links from the Degen Team sign-in page. A reset SMS is sent only when the request matches a phone number on file for an employee account.

Message Types

  • Employee invite links and account setup links.
  • Password reset links and account security notices.
  • Team portal notifications, including schedule, shift, and staff operations updates.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by employee activity and operational need. Most employees should receive fewer than four messages per month, with additional messages only when they request account access or password reset links.

Opt Out and Help

Reply STOP to opt out of non-essential operational SMS. Reply HELP for help. Employees can also contact a Degen Collectibles manager or email info@degencollectibles.com for SMS support.

Rates and Carrier Notice

Message and data rates may apply. Message delivery is not guaranteed. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Employee mobile numbers are used for Degen Team portal access, account security, and internal operational notifications. Mobile information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Access is limited to authorized Degen Collectibles personnel and service providers that operate the team portal and SMS delivery systems.

Sample Messages

  • Degen Team invite: https://ops.degencollectibles.com/team/invite/accept/[token] Expires in 24 hours. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Degen Team password reset: https://ops.degencollectibles.com/team/password/reset/[token] Expires in 60 minutes. Ignore this if you did not request it.
