Degen Collectibles QuickBooks Integration Terms

Effective date: May 14, 2026

Use of the Integration

The QuickBooks integration is provided for Degen Collectibles internal bookkeeping, reconciliation, financial reporting, and accounting operations. Only authorized owners, administrators, and finance personnel may connect a QuickBooks company or access imported QuickBooks data.

Authorization

A QuickBooks company connection must be authorized through Intuit's OAuth consent flow. Users must only connect QuickBooks companies that they are authorized to administer for Degen Collectibles.

Permitted Data Use

QuickBooks data is used only to support business workflows inside the Degen Collectibles operations portal. The integration may not be used to sell, disclose, or transfer QuickBooks data to unauthorized parties.

Availability and Errors

The application logs sync errors and exposes operational failures to authorized staff so issues can be reviewed. Degen Collectibles may disable or revoke the integration if it is no longer needed, if access is no longer authorized, or if a security concern is identified.

Disconnecting

Authorized users may disconnect the integration by revoking access in QuickBooks or by contacting Degen Collectibles operations support. See the data deletion and disconnect instructions.

Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to info@degencollectibles.com.
