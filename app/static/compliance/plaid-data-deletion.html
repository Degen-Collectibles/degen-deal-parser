Degen Collectibles Plaid Data Deletion and Disconnect

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Disconnecting Plaid

Authorized Degen Collectibles administrators may disconnect a Plaid bank connection by revoking the connection through Plaid, through the connected financial institution where available, or by requesting disconnection from Degen Collectibles operations support.

Requesting Deletion

To request deletion of stored Plaid integration data that is no longer required for bookkeeping, tax, audit, operational, or legal purposes, email info@degencollectibles.com with the account or institution name, requester name, and the type of data to delete or disconnect.

What We Delete or Revoke

  • Plaid access tokens and connection metadata for the disconnected account.
  • Imported bank-feed records that are not needed for bookkeeping, tax, audit, legal, or operational purposes.
  • Temporary exports or troubleshooting files that are no longer needed.

Retention Limits

Some financial records may need to be retained for tax, audit, dispute, legal, fraud-prevention, or legitimate business recordkeeping obligations. When full deletion is not available for those reasons, Degen Collectibles will restrict access and stop future Plaid syncs for the disconnected account.

Response Time

Degen Collectibles will review deletion and disconnect requests within a commercially reasonable timeframe and will prioritize requests involving security concerns.
