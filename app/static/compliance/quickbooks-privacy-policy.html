Degen Collectibles QuickBooks Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

This policy explains how Degen Collectibles uses data obtained through the QuickBooks Online API for the Degen Collectibles operations portal.

Purpose of the Integration

The QuickBooks integration is used to support internal bookkeeping, reconciliation, financial reporting, and operational accounting workflows. It helps authorized Degen Collectibles personnel compare QuickBooks accounting records with Discord deal logs, TikTok Shop sales, Shopify sales, inventory records, expenses, and payout data.

QuickBooks Data We May Access

Depending on the scopes approved by the business owner, the application may access accounting and reporting data needed for reconciliation, such as accounts, transactions, vendors, customers, reports, company metadata, and related bookkeeping records. The application does not use the QuickBooks Payments API to process card payments.

How Data Is Used

  • Import and classify bookkeeping transactions for internal accounting.
  • Match accounting rows against internal sales, inventory, and deal records.
  • Produce internal financial dashboards and exception reports.
  • Investigate reconciliation mismatches and operational errors.

Data Sharing

Degen Collectibles does not sell QuickBooks data and does not provide third parties with access to QuickBooks data except service providers that operate the application infrastructure under Degen Collectibles control, or when required by law.

Data Storage and Security

Production traffic is served over HTTPS. Access to bookkeeping and finance features is role-based and limited to authorized staff. API credentials and secrets are stored in environment configuration rather than source code. OAuth tokens, when stored, are protected from casual access and are rotated or revoked if compromise is suspected.

Retention and Deletion

Accounting records are retained only as needed for bookkeeping, tax, audit, legal, and operational purposes. QuickBooks connections can be revoked when they are no longer needed. See the data deletion and disconnect instructions.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the QuickBooks integration can be sent to info@degencollectibles.com.
