Auto-Categorized

The app assigns accounting categories from matched app transactions, Chase merchant categories, and business-specific bank-description rules.
1 Inventory first

Zelle, Venmo, PayPal, eBay, card-show seller payments, wires, and cash withdrawals are treated as inventory-related unless a stronger rule wins.

2 Operating expenses split out

PSA, shipping, payroll, taxes, software, travel, lodging, fuel, meals, rent, and supplies get their own buckets for export.

3 Unclear stays explicit

Paper checks and bank lines without enough payee detail stay Uncategorized instead of being silently guessed.
