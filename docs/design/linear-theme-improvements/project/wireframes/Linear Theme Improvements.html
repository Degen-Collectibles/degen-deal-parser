Degen Eye — Icon Concepts
5 directions for the center button icon. All shown at FAB size (46px) and small nav size (22px) so you can judge readability at both scales.
1 · Classic Eye
Simple open eye. Universally readable. Clean and iconic.
Safe · clear at any size
2 · Scanner Eye
Eye with corner brackets — feels like a card scanner / camera mode.
Ties into card scanning
3 · Live Eye
Eye with a pulsing live-dot in the corner — signals real-time monitoring.
Inherits your db-live dot pattern
4 · Degen Diamond
A diamond shape (crypto/degen culture shorthand) with a pupil center. Bold + on-brand.
Unique · memorable · very "degen"
5 · Hex Eye
Eye inside a hexagon — nods to trading card geometry and collectible grading slabs.
Feels like a PSA slab / card grade mark
